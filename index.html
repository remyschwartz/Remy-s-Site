Remy Schwartz is a producer and reporter living in New York City. He graduated from Hampshire College in May 2013 with a BA in Journalism and Media Studies. He produces video, radio, writing, and web. He grew up in Chicago, Illinois, where he developed an affinity for large dogs and city biking.

Co-Founder/Managing Editor, Switchboard Magazine

Amherst, MA • September 2012 — May 2013

I worked with my partner Duncan Sullivan to develop the tone, content model, and operational structure of the magazine. I oversaw a designer and programmer throughout the construction of the site and I functioned as the Managing Editor for the five months Switchboard published content. I produced or co-produced more than 30 multimedia during that period.
